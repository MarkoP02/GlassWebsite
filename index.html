Types of massage

Sports massage

Sports massage is reported to have many beneficial effects in athletes.
It can be used pre-performance, post-performance, during training, or for rehabilitation.
From elite athletes to recreational exercisers, it is a popular choice.

Aromatherapy massage

Aromatherapy massages are best for people who want to have an emotional healing component to their massage. Aromatherapy massages combine soft, gentle pressure with the use of essential oils.

Deep tissue massage

During a deep tissue massage, your massage therapist will use slow strokes and deep finger pressure to relieve tension from the deepest layers of your muscles and connective tissues.
